Cain Road Area Neighborhood Association

The Cain Road Area Neighborhood Association (CRANA) is comprised of approximately 800 households. Membership is open to all persons 18 years of age and older residing in CRANA as well as any legal entity owning property or a business is welcomed. Annual dues are just $5.00 and go to support programs like the Thurston County Food Project, as well as events like the candidate forum and the Capital City Marathon. The map of the area and a description are available here. Within the neighborhood we have two parks, Watershed and McGrath Woods; one school, Washington Middle School; and, three churches. The Association was established in 1991 and is a Recognized Neighborhood Association with the City of Olympia.
